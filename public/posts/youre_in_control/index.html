You’re in control

I believe one of the best tools to counter anxiety disorders is to finally understand that you’re ultimately in control.

Your mind is not the pilot, you are.

One of the best strategies that have helped me achieve my goal of controlling this Inner Demon is to assume control of the mind through voice and not the mind itself.

The way this works is, instead of trying to rationalize yourself out of that state of panic using your anxiety poisoned mind, you use your voice to find rational paths through your thoughts. Why this works for me is, one, it gets my focus out of that endless loop of anxiety thoughts and two, my voice cannot be contaminated by anxiety.

Tell yourself how the fears you’re having are not rational or if they are, the solution to best deal with it instead of using your poisoned mind.

Vocalizing alongside [[Anxiety is like Pennywise]] has helped me tremendously in controlling this monster within and I hope it does for you too.
